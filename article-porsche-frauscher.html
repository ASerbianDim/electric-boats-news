🚤 Porsche Enters the Electric Boat Market

LAUNCH

March 3, 2026 | By Editor | 4 min read

Porsche Engineering has partnered with Austrian boat builder Frauscher to create the 850 Fantom Air - an electric boat powered by components from the Porsche Macan electric vehicle.

Key Specifications

Power400 kW (536 hp)
Battery100 kWh (Porsche Macan components)
Range100 km at cruising speed
Top Speed90 km/h
Charging800V fast-charging capable
PriceStarts at €500,000

What Makes It Special

The 850 Fantom Air shares major components with Porsche's electric cars, including:

  • Battery system - Same 800V architecture as the Taycan and Macan
  • Power electronics - Proven inverter technology
  • Thermal management - Advanced cooling systems

Market Impact

Porsche's entry into the electric boat market signals a major shift. As automotive giants bring their EV expertise to marine applications, we can expect:

  • Rapid technology improvement
  • Economies of scale driving down costs
  • Improved charging infrastructure
  • Higher quality standards

Competition

The electric boat market is heating up. Key competitors include:

  • Ray - Swedish startup with sleek designs
  • X Shore - Norwegian electric boats
  • Silent-Yachts - Solar-powered catamarans
  • Axopar - Finnish boat builder going electric
