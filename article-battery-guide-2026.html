Everything You Need to Know About Marine Batteries

BATTERY GUIDE

March 4, 2026 | 8 min read

Choosing the right battery is the most important decision when going electric. This guide covers everything you need to know about marine batteries in 2026.

Battery Types Compared

Lithium-Ion (Li-ion)

Most popular choice. Excellent energy density, long lifespan, fast charging.

Energy Density150-250 Wh/kg
Lifespan2,000-3,000 cycles
Cost€500-€1,000/kWh
Charging Time4-8 hours

Solid-State (NEW 2026)

Game changer! Higher density, safer, longer life. Just hitting the market.

Energy Density300-400 Wh/kg
Lifespan5,000+ cycles
Cost€1,500-€2,000/kWh
Charging Time2-4 hours

LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate)

Safe & affordable. Great for budget builds, slightly heavier.

Energy Density100-160 Wh/kg
Lifespan3,000-5,000 cycles
Cost€300-€600/kWh
Charging Time4-6 hours

Calculate Your Range Needs

Formula: Range = (Battery kWh × 0.7) ÷ (Boat weight in tons × 0.5)

Example: 100kWh battery, 2 ton boat = (100 × 0.7) ÷ (2 × 0.5) = 70 nm range

Charging Infrastructure

Marinas across Europe now offer fast charging. Look for:

  • AC Slow: 7-22kW (overnight)
  • DC Fast: 50-150kW (2-4 hours)
