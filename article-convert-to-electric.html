Convert Your Boat to Electric

Complete DIY Conversion Guide

Converting your existing gas boat to electric can cost $10,000-$50,000 but saves money long-term. Here's how.

Step 1: Assess Your Boat

Weight, size, and hull type matter. Lightweight fiberglass boats convert best. Avoid heavy steel hulls.

Step 2: Choose Your System

  • Direct Drive: Most efficient, replaces engine directly
  • Pod Drive: Great for sailboats, compact
  • Outboard: Easiest to install, portable

Step 3: Select Battery

Li-ion: 150-250 Wh/kg, 2,000+ cycles
LFP: 100-160 Wh/kg, 3,000+ cycles, cheaper

Estimated Costs

  • Motor: $3,000-$15,000
  • Battery: $5,000-$25,000
  • Charger: $1,000-$3,000
  • Installation: $2,000-$5,000
  • Total: $10,000-$50,000
