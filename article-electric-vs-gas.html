Electric Boat vs Gas Boat

Which is Right for You in 2026?

Making the switch from gas to electric? Here's a complete breakdown of how they compare.

FactorElectricGas
Fuel Cost/mile$0.15-0.30$1.50-3.00
Maintenance/year$200-500$1,500-4,000
Range20-100 nm200-500 nm
Top Speed30-90 knots50-100+ knots
NoiseSilent80-100 dB
Lifespan10-20 years15-30 years
Purchase Price$15K-500K$10K-1M+

Verdict

Choose Electric if: You boat in protected waters, prioritize silence, want low maintenance, and short trips.

Choose Gas if: You need long range, high speed, or boat in remote areas.

The break-even point typically comes after 3-5 years due to fuel and maintenance savings.
