The Future of Emissions-Free Boating

Solar panels on boats can extend range indefinitely in sunny conditions. But is solar right for you?

How Solar Works on Boats

Solar panels charge the batteries during the day, extending range. A typical setup includes:

  • 500W-2kW solar panels
  • Solar charge controller
  • Large battery bank (100-200kWh)

✅ Pros

  • Free infinite range in daylight
  • No charging infrastructure needed
  • Zero emissions always
  • Works while anchored

❌ Cons

  • Expensive initial setup
  • Weather dependent
  • Adds weight
  • Limited top speed
