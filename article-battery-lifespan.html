What Affects Battery Life?

  • Depth of discharge: Keep between 20-80% for best longevity
  • Charging speed: Fast charging generates heat = faster degradation
  • Temperature: Extreme cold/hot speeds up aging
  • Storage: Store at 50% charge if not using for months

Pro Tips for Maximum Lifespan

  • Charge to 80% for daily use, 100% only for long trips
  • Never let battery drop below 20%
  • Use quality marine-grade chargers
  • Keep batteries at room temperature when possible

When to Replace?

When capacity drops below 70% of original. A 100kWh battery that now only holds 70kWh should be replaced.

Warranty

Most marine batteries come with 5-10 year warranties. Premium brands like Torqeedo offer up to 10 years or 3,000 cycles.
