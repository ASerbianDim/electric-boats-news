Electric Boat Incentives

Grants & Tax Credits by Country

Many governments offer incentives to promote clean marine transportation. Here's what's available in 2026.

🇺🇸 United States

Up to $7,500 federal tax credit

Clean Vehicle Credit for new electric boats. Some states offer additional rebates.

🇳🇴 Norway

VAT exemption + NOx tax waiver

One of the best incentives worldwide. Electric boats are essentially tax-free.

🇸🇪 Sweden

25% climate bonus

Subsidy up to SEK 100,000 for electric boat purchases.

🇩🇪 Germany

Up to €5,000 environment bonus

Federal and state incentives for electric propulsion.

🇫🇷 France

Up to €10,000 bonus écologique

Available for both new purchases and conversions.

🇬🇧 UK

Up to £5,000 grant

Plug-in Boat Grant covers 20-30% of purchase price.

Tip: Check your local maritime authority for additional regional programs!
